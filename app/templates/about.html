About Kemp Po

I got a Bachelor's in Computer Science with a specialization in Data Science and Analytics at Ateneo de Manila University. Currently, I work at First Circle, a startup working towards financial inclusion for small and medium enterprises, as a Data Analyst.

I am a data person and problem solver that enjoys developing data products where they are needed. I'm passionate about leveraging data to improve people's lives.

About this website

Initially, this was just supposed to be a project never to be deployed, but instead just a way for me to start learning web frameworks on languages that I'm much more proficient in.

Surely, there were many other much simpler ways of creating this blog, but it is another way for me to sharpen my skills and expand my knowledge.

At some point, I realized that I had thoughts and practices that I wanted to share to the world especially in the budding data community in the Philippines, where many people come from the non-CS side of things.
